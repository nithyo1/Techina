Wonderland Wisdom

About this project:

This project helps you with your long term dopamine levels, specially focused on helping women with ADHD. With the Mad Hatter's Mood Checker, the user can track their moods, which can make help make long term conclusions. In turn calendar can store those check-in's. The calendar displays the check-ins as a color, with moods rangeing from red to green. At the heart of our idea, there is the Jabberwocky Generator. For long term benefits, it is important to almost "trick" or suprise your brain. Randomizing whether you do a dopamine charged activity each day or not, will help achive a healthy levels of dopamine in the long term.
